Settings

Spotify not connected

Free Spotify app credentials (client-credentials — no login). Surfaces tracks beyond your library and flags which you already own.

TIDAL not connected

Free TIDAL developer app (developer.tidal.com → create an app → client credentials). Surfaces catalog tracks by artist. Note: still being verified against the live API.

Do Not Play

One per line — an artist, a title, or “Artist - Title”. Matching tracks never appear in suggestions, and the deck warns if one is playing.

Waveforms

Waveforms + structure (intro / drop / outro) are built on the fly and cached as you go. Optionally pre-load your whole library ahead of a gig (runs in the background, resumable).

Song popularity

World popularity (Deezer global rank) is fetched for suggestions as you play and factored into ranking — crowd-pleasers rank higher. Optionally pre-load your whole library (background, resumable, slower).
